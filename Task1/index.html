About

Villages are found to be more peaceful, calm, quiet, and full of greenery, where one can always breathe
fresh air and stay healthy without any pollution problems. It also plays a major role in maintaining the
ecological balance of the environment.The villages are the souls of our country, and more than 60% of the

major role in maintaining the ecological balance of the environment. Villages are mostly covered with trees
